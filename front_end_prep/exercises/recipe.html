Pear and Bacon Grilled Cheese

Ingredients

2 TBS unsalted butter

2 TBS currant or fig jam

2 slices white sandwich bread

2 slices Cheddar

2 slices cooked bacon

1/4 small pear, thinly sliced

Directions

1. Melt the butter in a small skillet over medium-low heat. Spread the jam on 1 slice of break and form a sandwhich with the cheese, bacon, and pear.

2. Cook the sandwich, covered, until the bread is toasted and the cheese is melted, 2-3 minutes per side.
